)
explicit

construct a rotor from configuration strings

Parameters
rotor_configConfiguration of the rotor represented by a string. Represents wires of the reflector, aligns the configuration with the alphabet, so 'a' will translate to the first character, 'b' to the second, etc.
notch_configconfiguration of the notches represented by a string. All the characters in the string will be interpreted as a notch, when the rotor advances past those characters, it will step the wheel to its left.
